My name is Dale Lama. The journey of my life begins in a small country Nepal where I was born and raised. Pure water, clean air, and breath-taking mountainous landscape of majestic nature and thousands of tourists from different cultural backgrounds every year from all over the world inspired me to explore something new all the time. In 1997, I moved to the United States. Everything altogether was a whole new world for me. I did not have any work experience in this fast-paced environment and I had to struggle a lot just to keep up with the living. I did not have a clear direction of my destination, and all I had was an idea of seeking better brought me here and will guide me further.

Ever since I enrolled in Emerging Media here at Hunter College, the dimension of my life has altogether changed. Media has taught me to explore the infinite possibilities around me by developing a meaningful connection in every digital image element. The color palette, shapes, and many layers further contribute to the idea of the everyday scene and ultimately interpret this moment into digital media. As I make progress that would then turn my intelligence into the skillful techniques.

I feel art is not just a mixture of color, shape, pattern, number, or texts but it is an ultimate expression. You can say things that cannot be said through words. These art forms give vivid emotions and unforgettable experiences. Art teaches me to see things from both inside and outside which gives me an ability to look at situations from different perspectives and choose the best solution.

I am an ambitious, determined, and well-organized artist. Planning, organizing, and overlooking strategies are among my strengths. I am a hardworking individual looking to find a niche in the New Media field. I still have a long way to go, but this is just the beginning of a bright and better future.
